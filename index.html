About Us

Welcome to Dakshina Delicacy! We take immense pride in delivering the authentic flavors of South India right to your doorstep. Indulge in a delightful journey through our exclusive range of handcrafted South Indian sweets, meticulously prepared with time-honored traditional ingredients. Each sweet is a harmonious blend of rich heritage and exquisite taste, crafted with love and passion by our expert artisans. From the heavenly aroma to the melt-in-your-mouth experience, every bite will transport you to the vibrant streets of South India. Treat yourself or surprise your loved ones with our tempting selection, and savor the sweetness of our cherished culinary legacy.

Featured Dishes

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

Unniyappam

Rs. 200

Unniyappam or Unni Appam are sweet fritters made with rice, banana, a few spices and coconut. This sweet snack is a popular one in the scenic state of Kerala in South India. This Unniyappam Recipe is quite a nutritious preparation, and is gluten free as well as vegan-friendly too.

Belagavi Kunda

Rs. 150

Experience Belagavi Kunda, a North Karnataka delight, crafted by reducing milk with caramelized sugar. Simple yet time-consuming, the heavenly taste is worth the patience. Try this blissful sweet treat today!

Kozhukatta

Rs. 250

Delight in the time-honored flavors of our Kozhukatta - a medley of melted jaggery, grated coconut, and aromatic spices like green cardamom and cumin seeds. Embrace the joy of learning and the warmth of cherished family traditions with each delectable dumpling, lovingly crafted to perfection.

Chiroti

Rs. 300

Indulge in Chiroti, a flaky South Indian dessert made with semolina and powdered sugar. A premium sweet for special occasions, served crushed with badam milk and powdered sugar. Try our detailed recipe now!

Dharwad Peda

Rs. 300

Experience the uniqueness of Dharwad Peda, a special sweet from Northern Karnataka. Made with paneer and milk, it features a distinct dark brown color and is rolled on sugar crystals for a delightful touch.

Rava Unda

Rs. 100

Experience the delectable taste of our Kerala Rava Unda - a unique sweet treat prepared with semolina and rich flavors. Perfect for special occasions, this quick and nutritious delicacy is loved by all ages. Try it now!

Unde

Deliciously sweet and aromatic South Indian laddoos, a traditional Indian dessert cherished for their rich flavors and melt-in-your-mouth texture.


Payasam

Indulge in the heavenly taste of South Indian payasam, a delightful rice and milk pudding infused with aromatic flavors, truly a heartwarming dessert for every occasion.
